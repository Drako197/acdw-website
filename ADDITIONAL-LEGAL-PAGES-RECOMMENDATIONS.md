## Important Legal Pages (Medium Priority)

### 4. **Cookie Policy** üü° **IMPORTANT**

**Why Essential:**
- **GDPR/CCPA compliance** - Required if you have EU/CA visitors
- **Transparency** - Shows what tracking you use
- **Legal requirement** - Many jurisdictions require disclosure
- **Best practice** - Builds trust

**What to Include:**
- What cookies you use (essential, analytics, marketing)
- Purpose of each cookie type
- Third-party cookies (Google reCAPTCHA, Stripe, Clerk, Analytics)
- How to manage/disable cookies
- Cookie consent mechanism (if required)
- Data retention periods

**Current Cookies/Tracking:**
- Google reCAPTCHA (bot protection)
- Stripe (payment processing)
- Clerk (authentication)
- Google Analytics (when implemented)
- Netlify (hosting/analytics)

**Where to Link:**
- Footer
- Cookie banner (if implemented)
- Privacy Policy (cross-reference)

**Estimated Time:** 1 hour
